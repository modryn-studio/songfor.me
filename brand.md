# Brand

---

## Positioning

- **What we are:** The only birthday song generator that actually captures who someone is — their quirks, their jokes, their story.
- **What we're not:** Another form where you type a name and pick "rock" or "pop."
- **One-liner:** "Gift cards are forgettable. This isn't."

---

## Voice

How the product sounds in UI copy, headings, CTAs, and error messages.

- Warm, celebratory, but never cheesy. Like a friend who gives great gifts, not a Hallmark card.
- Short sentences. Conversational. Reads like a text from someone who knows you.
- Confident about what it does ("this will make them cry happy tears") without overselling the tech.
- Never use: "AI-powered", "leverage", "revolutionary", "seamless", "unlock", "utilize", "cutting-edge", "game-changer"

---

## The User

A mom scrolling her phone at 10pm realizing her kid's friend's birthday party is tomorrow and she forgot a gift. Or a 30-something who just saw their best friend's birthday post on Instagram and wants to do something that actually means something — not another gift card. They're not planners. They're impulse gifters with good hearts and no time.

---

## Visual Rules

- Color mode: Light base with warm tones. Feels like a celebration, not a SaaS dashboard.
- Fonts: Inter (body, clean and friendly) + Instrument Serif (headlines, warm and personal)
- Motion: Confetti burst on song delivery. Gentle pulse on "your song is being crafted" waiting state. Nothing else.
- Avoid: No stock photos, no generic music note clipart, no dark mode (this is a gift — it should feel bright), no popups, no cookie banners at launch

---

## Color System

| Name      | Hex       | Role                                                            |
| --------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Coral** | `#FF6B6B` | Primary — CTAs, logomark, key UI moments                        |
| **Gold**  | `#FFD93D` | Celebration accent — confetti, highlights, delivery states only |
| **Cream** | `#FFFAF5` | Background base                                                 |
| **Ink**   | `#1C1410` | Body text — warm near-black, never cold                         |
| **Dusk**  | `#9C8070` | Muted — secondary text, borders, placeholders, dividers         |

Rules:

- No cool colors anywhere. No blue, no purple, no pure black, no pure white.
- Gold is sparingly used — celebration moments only. Overuse makes it childish.
- Songful's territory: pink-to-purple gradients, cool/clean SaaS aesthetic. Stay warm.

---

## Logomark

**Direction:** Single letterform — the `s` set in Instrument Serif, coral on transparent background. No container. The terminal treatment (ends of the `s` strokes) must be distinctive — slightly extended or with a subtle drawn quality — to separate it from a bare typeface character. This is where the mark becomes ours.

**Primary color:** Coral `#FF6B6B`

**Background:** Transparent — no circle, no badge, no container of any kind.

**Future-proofing:** No birthday-specific imagery. The mark must hold if the product expands to anniversaries, graduations, or corporate gifting. A letterform is content-agnostic.

**Competitor exclusions (Songful's occupied territory — avoid entirely):**

- Pink-to-purple gradient fill
- Circle badge container
- Music note icon
- Polished, geometric, constructed SaaS aesthetic

**Anti-patterns (category clichés — avoid entirely):**

- Music notes, treble clef, vinyl, headphones, piano keys
- Horizontal waveform bars (Spotify/ElevenLabs territory)
- Gift ribbon or bow (birthday-specific, not future-proof)
- Any gradient fill

## Emotional Arc

What a visitor feels at each stage — land, read, scroll, convert.

- Land: "Wait — I can make a real song for them? Right now?"
- Intake: "This is fun. It feels like I'm writing them a love letter."
- Waiting: "I can't wait to hear this. I hope it's good."
- Delivery: "Oh my god. This is so them. I'm sending this right now."
- Share: "Everyone needs to hear this. I'm posting this."

## Copy Examples

Real copy to use as reference when writing UI text.

- Hero: "Make them a birthday song they'll never forget."
- Subhead: "Tell us about them. We'll write the lyrics, compose the music, and deliver a one-of-a-kind song in minutes."
- CTA: "Start their song →"
- Waiting state: "Your song is being crafted with love and a little bit of magic ✨"
- Delivery email subject: "🎂 [Name]'s birthday song is ready!"
- Song page: "This song was made for [Name], because [they deserve something that's actually personal]."
- Footer: "Built by Luke. Because gift cards are boring."
- Error: "Something went sideways. Try again — [Name] is worth it."
